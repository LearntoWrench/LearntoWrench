Learn to Wrench FAQ

What is Learn to Wrench?

Learn to Wrench is an educational service that teaches automotive diagnosis, repair, and maintenance. Students inquire about specific jobs they would like to learn on their car. Each class is unique and tailored to the specific repair(s) and diagnosis a student wants or needs to learn at that point in time. 

Learn to Wrench makes car repair education more accessible, and more student centered while also being more convenient. Inquire for a class for when YOU are free and have time to learn instead of when a class schedule says so. 

Where can I take a Learn to Wrench class?

Learn to Wrench mobilly services most towns and cities near Amherst, MA, 01002. Learn to Wrench can come to your location to teach classes on most vehicles.

How does Learn to Wrench work?

1. Inquire for a class with our form, located in the top menu or here.
2. Learn to Wrench will get back to you with a determination if we are able to teach the class, a price and an estimated amount of time it will take to teach the requested jobs. Along with this will be a time and place based off the answers on the inquiry form. 
3. Once you get a response from LTW confirm the class or you can decline the class as well if you have any issues with it or the cost. 
4. If you accept the class we will meet and get you turning wrenches at the location time and date we agreed on!

Who supplies the parts if they are necessary?

Students supply the parts. Learn to Wrench can assist sourcing and getting the right parts if necessary!
